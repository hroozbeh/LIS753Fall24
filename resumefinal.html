As previously mentioned on my career goals page, I have worked in personal injury law firms and I have some experience in family law. The last position I held, before starting the SOIS program at Dominican University, was in a high-volume personal injury law firm. Some of my duties for this role included: drafting discovery for MVA, premises liability, animal bite, and child abuse cases; Drafted motions, interrogatories, pleadings; drafted 90c Packets for arbitration; drafted various letters to clients; requested medical records and bills, DCFS reports, court reporters and translators for depositions; inquired about client and defendant information from insurance companies.

During my time at Valparaiso University Law School, I worked as an intern in the General Civil Practice Clinic for one year. My experience and duties in the clinic consisted of conducting initial interviews for divorce, guardianship, adoption, name change, minor car accident, and small claims cases; wrote complaints for divorce, status report on guardianship, and summons; attended settlement meetings and depositions; observed court proceedings in divorce, domestic violence, and guardianship matters.
